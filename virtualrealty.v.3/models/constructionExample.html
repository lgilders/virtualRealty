The Palmetto by Sabal Homes

3 bedrooms | 2 bathrooms | 1750 - 2500 sq. ft. | Learn More

The Palmetto gives you spacious one-story living with the option of adding a second floor for even more space. An inviting foyer leads you down the hall past the second and third bedroom and then opens up into a large living space. No need to miss out on the action when preparing for dinner, as the well-designed kitchen with roomy pantry overlooks the family room.

Want more outdoor space? Just screen in the covered rear porch to enjoy the great outdoors, or you can opt to make it into a sunroom. The master suite is a quiet retreat with its own bathroom, featuring raised vanities, a private water closet, and walk-in closet.

You can keep the Palmetto as a one story home or add an upstairs to give you more than 2,200 square feet! Choose between the optional bonus room, optional bonus room plus full bath, or optional bonus room plus full bath and fourth bedroom. You can really make this home your own with the many customization options the Palmetto offers.
